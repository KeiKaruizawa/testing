Computer ~ An electronic device that processes information
Internet ~ A global network connecting millions of devices
Keyboard ~ A device with keys used for typing
Monitor ~ A screen that displays visual information
Smartphone ~ A mobile device with advanced computing features
Tablet ~ A flat touchscreen device larger than a phone
Laptop ~ A portable computer you can carry anywhere
Software ~ Programs and applications that run on computers
Hardware ~ The physical parts of a computer system
Website ~ A collection of pages accessed through the internet
Robot ~ A machine capable of carrying out tasks automatically
Bluetooth ~ Wireless technology for short-range connections
Password ~ A secret code used to protect your accounts
Download ~ Transferring data from the internet to your device
Touchscreen ~ A display that responds to finger touch
Processor ~ The brain of a computer that performs calculations
Digital ~ Information stored in electronic binary form
Scanner ~ A device that converts physical documents to digital
Printer ~ A machine that produces paper copies from digital files
Algorithm ~ A set of rules for solving problems or tasks
