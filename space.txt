Galaxy ~ A massive system of stars and planets
Planet ~ A large celestial body orbiting a star
Rocket ~ A vehicle designed to travel beyond Earth
Asteroid ~ A rocky object floating through space
Meteor ~ A space rock that burns bright entering atmosphere
Satellite ~ A device orbiting Earth for communication
Astronaut ~ A person trained to travel in space
Comet ~ An icy body with a glowing tail in the sky
Eclipse ~ When one celestial body blocks another from view
Crater ~ A bowl-shaped hole on a planet's surface
Nebula ~ A cloud of dust and gas where stars are born
Orbit ~ The curved path an object takes around another
Gravity ~ The force that pulls objects toward each other
Telescope ~ An instrument used to see distant stars
Supernova ~ An exploding star that releases incredible energy
Universe ~ Everything that exists including space and time
Martian ~ Something or someone from the planet Mars
Starship ~ A spacecraft designed for interstellar travel
Moonwalk ~ When astronauts walk on the lunar surface
Alien ~ A being from another world or planet
